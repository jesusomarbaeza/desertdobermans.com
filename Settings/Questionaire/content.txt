TITLE: Puppy Adoption Questionnaire

INTRO: This questionnaire is designed to help you prepare for the exciting journey of welcoming a Doberman puppy into your life. It also allows us to better understand your needs and provide any additional information or guidance to ensure a smooth and successful transition for both you and your new companion.

CATEGORY: Breed Awareness & Research
Q: What specific Doberman traits (positive or negative) influenced your decision to choose this breed?
Q: How does the Doberman's reputation as a "Velcro dog" fit into your personal lifestyle?
Q: What are three things you've learned about Dobermans that surprised you during your research?

CATEGORY: Experience & Philosophy
Q: Can you describe your history with working breeds or high-energy dogs?
Q: How has your past dog ownership shaped your current approach to training and discipline?
Q: What is your philosophy on correcting unwanted behaviors in a powerful, sensitive breed?

CATEGORY: Physical & Mental Stimulation
Q: Dobermans need "jobs." What daily mental tasks will you provide to prevent boredom and destruction?
Q: How many hours of focused physical exercise can you realistically provide every single day?
Q: How do you plan to handle the high energy levels of a Doberman during its first two years?

CATEGORY: Living Environment
Q: Is your yard fully fenced? Please describe the height and material of your perimeter.
Q: Will the dog live primarily indoors with the family or in an outdoor kennel?
Q: Are there any major life changes (moving, new job, baby) expected in the next three years?

CATEGORY: Training & Socialization
Q: Which professional trainers or facilities in your area have you already researched for puppy classes?
Q: How will you socialize your puppy to ensure they are neutral and confident around strangers?
Q: Are you prepared to handle "rebellion" stages during the Doberman's adolescence?

CATEGORY: Health & Wellness
Q: Are you familiar with DCM and vWD? How will you monitor for breed-specific health issues?
Q: Do you commit to keeping this dog on a regular preventative care schedule with a vet?
Q: What is your plan for grooming tasks like weekly nail dremeling and ear cleaning?

CATEGORY: Financial Commitment
Q: Are you prepared for the high cost of quality large-breed nutrition and supplements?
Q: Have you researched pet insurance or set aside an emergency fund for unexpected surgeries?
Q: Are you aware of the costs for professional training, which is often essential for this breed?

CATEGORY: Household Dynamics
Q: Please list the ages of everyone in your home and their previous experience with large dogs.
Q: How many hours per day will the puppy be left alone, and where will they stay while you are out?
Q: How will you ensure the safety of other pets or small children in the home with a large puppy?

CATEGORY: Long-term Expectations
Q: What is your plan for the dog if you can no longer care for it due to an emergency?
Q: Do you have any intention of breeding this dog, or do you agree to a spay/neuter contract?
Q: What role does this dog play in your life: protector, athlete, or strictly a companion?

CATEGORY: Potential Challenges
Q: How will you manage a 90lb dog that may develop protective instincts or "same-sex aggression"?
Q: Are you prepared for the time commitment of a dog that requires constant human interaction?
Q: What is your biggest concern or fear regarding owning a Doberman?
